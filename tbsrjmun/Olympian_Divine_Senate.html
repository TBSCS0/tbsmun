    Isabel Martins (TBSRJ)
    Head Chair

    Hello, everyone, my name is Isabel Martins and I am a Junior at The British School Rio de Janeiro. I am beyond thrilled to serve as Head Chair for the Olympian Divine Senate alongside Duda. MUN has become one of my greatest passions; providing me with incomparable memories and improving my public speaking and researching skills significantly. TBSRJMUN has a very special place in my heart as it was one of my first conferences as a delegate, and my first conference as a chair. Therefore, I will strive to make your experience as a delegate as amazing as mine was back in 2018. I promise to make this committee as pleasant as possible. I sincerely hope that delegates not only learn from this experience but also have fun during it. Outside of MUN, I love to travel, dance, and spend time with my friends and family. Do not hesitate to contact me if you have any doubts or just want to chat, I am here to help! I cannot wait to meet all of you!

    Eduarda Hamdar de Castilho (ISC)
    Chair

    Hi, everyone! My name is Eduarda, a rising Junior at the International School of Curitiba, and I am beyond honoured to be chairing the Olympian Divine Senate at TBSRJMUN XXI alongside Isabel. I have been participating in MUN since the 7th grade and enjoy it greatly as I get the opportunity to make new friends and learn about eccentric topics. I believe MUN is the ideal place to learn about what is going on in our world and have fun while doing so. My favorite parts about MUN are definitely, Crises and, of course, meeting new people. I am really looking forward to seeing the Olympian Divine Senate debating and how delegates will interpret your respective figures. Besides MUN and school activities, outside of school, you can find me watching a good sitcom, listening to music, obsessing over any album Jack Antonoff has produced, traveling, or hanging out with my friends. Can't wait to see you all at TBSRJMUN XII. If you have any questions or need help, feel free to contact me!
