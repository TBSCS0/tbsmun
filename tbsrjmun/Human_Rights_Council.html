    Luisa Bravo (TBSRJ)
    Chair

    Hello, all, my name is Luisa Bravo and I am a freshman at The British School of Rio de Janeiro’s Urca Site. It is truly an honour to be serving as Chair of the Human Rights Council! Chairing this committee at TBSRJMUN XXI is extremely fulfilling, especially as my first conference was in TBSRJMUN XVIII’s Junior Human Rights Council. MUN has taught me countless skills that I apply to my daily activities. Because of MUN, I am a more confident speaker, an avid writer, and a more voracious debater. As a Chair, my aim is to guarantee a safe environment where all delegates feel welcome to explore their abilities and share their wildest points and arguments. Outside of MUN, I love reading and writing, listening to my favourite bands, and doing any activities involving water. I look forward to meeting future diplomats and watching the prodigious debate that will surely take place! If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, don’t hesitate to contact me!
