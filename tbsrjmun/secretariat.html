    Chief Operations Officer and Press Director - Mariano Pucciarelli

    Dear delegates, fellow Leadership Team members, and Advisors, my name is Mariano Pucciarelli and I am beyond honoured to be TBSRJMUN XXI’s Chief Operations Officer and Press Corps Director. Since my early years at The British School of Rio de Janeiro, I became extremely involved with Model United Nations and fell deeper in love with it with each passing conference. Nowadays, as a Junior at TBSRJ, I look back and feel grateful for the marvellous collection of memories and friends MUN has given me throughout the years. Our attendees’ experience and well-being is our number one priority. Thus, TBSRJMUN’s main goal is to create a proper and safe environment where all delegates can flourish their skills and have an amazing time while doing so. We are working to make sure you all leave our conference with the fondest of memories. Thus, do not worry if this is your first or twentieth conference, our entire team is always here to help in any way needed. We will have a myriad of surprises and innovations to facilitate your stay at Rio de Janeiro and our school, we cannot wait to show you all! Hence, I invite you all to use this conference to make new friends, learn more about the world, and, most importantly, grow not only as MUNers but as individuals. For those of you coming outside of Rio de Janeiro, I wish you an amazing stay in the Cidade Maravilhosa. If you have any queries regarding our conference’s logistics or press team, do not hesitate to contact me, I will be more than eager to guide you through our processes. See you all in September!

    Secretary-General - Rafaela Pires

    Esteemed delegates and fellow faculty advisors, my name is Rafaela Pires, I am a junior at TBSRJ and I am beyond honoured to be serving as your Secretary-General for the twenty-first edition of TBSRJMUN! I quite cannot believe almost six years have passed since the first conference I ever attended; if I could travel back then to give myself one piece of advice, it would be: 'not everything has to be perfectly scrutinised, or perfectly written - what makes you a diplomat of character is to speak from the heart.' As an ambitious delegate, this is probably a sentence you would really like to relate to, which is why I invite you to TBSRJMUN! I am thrilled to be having the opportunity to guide our leadership team along the preparation of a very successful conference. Lastly, please don't be shy to ask questions or express any doubts - I will always be available to support you! See you soon, and let's make this edition of TBSRJMUN truly memorable!
