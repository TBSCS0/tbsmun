    Security Council

    Meet TBSRJMUN XXI's Security Council
    Rafaela Pires
    Secretary-General

    Esteemed delegates and fellow faculty advisors, my name is Rafaela Pires, I am a junior at TBSRJ and I am beyond honoured to be serving as your Secretary-General for the twenty-first edition of TBSRJMUN! I quite cannot believe almost six years have passed since the first conference I ever attended; if I could travel back then to give myself one piece of advice, it would be: 'Not everything has to be perfectly scrutinised, or perfectly written - what makes you a diplomat of character is to speak from the heart.' As an ambitious delegate, this is probably a sentence you would really like to relate to, which is why I invite you to TBSRJMUN! I am thrilled to be having the opportunity to guide our leadership team along the preparation for a very successful conference. Lastly, please don't be shy to ask questions or express any doubts - I will always be available to support you! See you soon, and let's make this edition of TBSRJMUN truly memorable!
