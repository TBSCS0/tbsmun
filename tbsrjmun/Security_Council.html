    Bernardo Grupenmacher
    Head Chair

    Hi, delegates, my name is Bernardo Grupenmacher and I am a rising Senior at the International School of Curitiba. I am very honored to be serving as Head Chair for the Security Council at this year's TBSRJMUN. Since my first conference, I was never able to stop doing MUN. It has taught me how to become a better public speaker and person and helped me delve into public relations and international affairs. When I first started MUN, I was a very shy delegate. I only made a few of my points and was afraid to talk. However, with time, I began developing my debating skills. As a Chair, I intend to pass my knowledge on debating and teach delegates skills they’ll take forever in life. To my future delegates, I hope you all become open-minded and passionate delegates, always aiming to defend your views. If you have any questions, concerns, or want to talk about the conference, feel free to reach out. I am very excited to meet all of you!

    ber.grupenmacher@gmail.com

    Giovana Lacerda
    Chair

    Hello, delegates! My name is Giovana and I am a sophomore at Colégio Leffler. I am beyond honored and excited to chair the Security Council. I started my MUN career in 2021 and as I participated in more MUN conferences I understood its procedure, skills, and that growth comes from practice. Debating, negotiating and writing resolutions have become some of my uttermost passions. In the world of debate, you get the best from both sides of life, academic challenges that make you learn, and friends that you take for the rest of your life. Overall, regardless if you are a first-timer or an experienced debater, you always have a lot to learn from your fellow delegates. As your chairs, we want to make this the most enriching and fun conference you can have! Count on us for anything you need. If you have any concerns do not hesitate to contact me, I will be glad to answer!

    gigigiovanarl@gmail.com
