    Gabriela Linhares (TBSRJ)
    Head Chair

    Hi, delegates! My name is Gabriela Linhares and I am a Junior at the British School Rio de Janeiro’s Barra site. I am beyond honoured to be chairing the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation Committee alongside Nicolle. I have been involved in MUN since 6th grade and it has become one of my favourite activities in and outside of school. MUN ignited my interest in foreign policy and diplomacy and helped me improve my public speaking skills immensely. I hope you all can have a similar experience on this committee. I hope this conference helps you realise how important diplomacy is and how it allows you to understand different points of view. Outside of MUN, you can find me watching films, reading novels, travelling, or learning new languages. Don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or concerns or just want to talk about movies or practice your Italian! I look forward to meeting all of you!

    gamlinhares@gmail.com

    Nicolle Malta (EAC)
    Chair

    Hi, delegates! My name is Nicolle Reis and I am currently a junior at Escola Americana de Campinas. First of all, I would like to say that I am deeply honored to be chairing the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation in this year's TBSRJMUN. Since the start of my high school years as a freshman, I was deeply intrigued by participating in debates related to international issues and debating in special committees. Besides the fun part of MUN, this activity has helped me grow my passion for international politics and law, improve my public speaking skills, and develop my quick thinking skills. As your chair, I hope I can help you grow your debating skills, write effective resolutions, and, of course, ensure a fun, enjoyable, and fruitful debate for all. I hope that our time together will help create great memories and leave long-lasting positive effects on your lives! If any questions arise or need someone to talk to before the conference, feel free to contact me. See you soon!
