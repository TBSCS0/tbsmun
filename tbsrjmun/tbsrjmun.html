TBSRJMUN XXI

15-17 September, 2023

About

Hosted and run yearly by students from The British School of Rio de Janeiro (TBSRJ) since 2002, TBSRJMUN welcomes you to its 21st edition. Every year, our school receives students from numerous educational institutions to engage in diplomacy and learn about the world’s latest disputes.

Being granted the privilege of being BRAMUN 2024's sister conference, TBSRJMUN XXI will consist of BRAMUN 2024's Leadership Team and be an opportunity for Chairs and Secretariat members to know each other better before BRAMUN.

In addition to our 12 committees, schools visiting the “Cidade Maravilhosa” will be able to participate in numerous other activities hosted by our conference. We cannot wait to see you all in September! If you have any queries, don’t hesitate to contact us.

Conference Structure

Offering a total of 12 committees, nine senior committees and three junior committees, those participating in TBSRJMUN XXI will have the opportunity to delve into a wide range of subjects including history and current international affairs.

In addition to our committees, delegates will be able to compete in groups along with their respective committees during our TBSRJMUN Cup, which will include a Knowledge Bowl, a Love Mail competition, and more!

History

Aiming to provide TBSRJ’s students with further learning opportunities, The British School, Rio de Janeiro Model United Nations was founded by David Williams in 2002. Since then, TBSRJMUN has kept growing to what is now its twenty-first edition. Hence, becoming a vital part of our school’s community.

Over the years, TBSRJMUN has received students from all over Brazil and we are extremely proud to be BRAMUN 2024’s sister conference. We are sure this conference will provide us all with amazing memories and the opportunity to impact a myriad of lives.

We are very proud to welcome you all to our beloved conference and cannot wait to see you very soon!
