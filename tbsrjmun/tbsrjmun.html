    TBSRJMUN XXI

    15-17 September, 2023

    About

    Hosted and run yearly by students from The British School of Rio de Janeiro (TBSRJ) since 2002,
    TBSRJMUN welcomes you to its 21st edition. Every year, our school receives students from numerous
    educational institutions to engage in diplomacy and learn about the world’s latest disputes.

    Being granted the privilege of being BRAMUN 2024's sister conference, TBSRJMUN XXI will consist of BRAMUN
    2024's Leadership Team and be an opportunity for Chairs and Secretariat members to know each other better
    before BRAMUN.

    In addition to our 12 committees, schools visiting the “Cidade Maravilhosa” will be able to participate in
    numerous other activities hosted by our conference. We cannot wait to see you all in September! If you have
    any queries, don’t hesitate to contact us.

    Conference Structure

    Offering a total of 12 committees, nine senior committees and three junior committees, those participating in TBSRJMUN XXI will have the opportunity to delve into a wide range of subjects including history and current international affairs. In addition to our committees, delegates will be able to compete in groups along with their respective committees during our TBSRJMUN Cup, which will include a Knowledge Bowl, a Love Mail competition, and more!
