    Gabriela Saute (PASPOA)
    Head Chair

    Hi, everyone! My name is Gabriela Saute and I am overly excited to be a chair at TBSRJMUN XXI. Currently a rising senior at the Pan American School of Porto Alegre, I have participated in TBSRJMUN as a delegate before and had a wonderful experience. Therefore, I'm thrilled to be back with another perspective. I've been involved in MUN since 7th grade, which has become a great passion of mine. I love speaking in public, learning new things, and sharing ideas with people from different backgrounds, as I believe that we learn the most from the people that are the most different from us. My favorite subject in school is history. Hence, historical committees are always very appealing to me. That is why I will be Head Chairing Margaret Thatcher's UK Parliament. Also known as “The Iron Lady”, Thatcher was a remarkable figure in British politics, to say the least, and the first female Prime Minister of the United Kingdom. I love The Crown, so you can find very good context on who she was and what she stood for if you do too! Outside of MUN, I love to listen to music, go to dance classes, play soccer, and go out to new restaurants. Don't hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or want to chat!

    Manuela Grimaldi (St. Nicholas School)
    Chair

    Hi, everyone! My name is Manuela Grimaldi and I am a rising Junior at St Nicholas School Pinheiros. I am delighted to be serving as a Chair for Margaret Thatcher’s UK Parliament at TBSRJMUN. My experience with conferences has been nothing short of amazing. For all first-time delegates, I hope me and Gabi can make TBSRJMUN XXI the best experience possible for you. Engaging in debates has always been a passion of mine. Therefore, being able to take part in conferences is a great opportunity to sharpen the skills necessary to be an avid debater. My journey within MUN has helped me grow confidence and shaped me into a more organised person. Furthermore, it has allowed me to meet new people and discuss pressing issues, always a bonus and important for school life. In my free time, I enjoy reading, playing beach tennis, going to the gym, and spending time with my friends and family. I am very excited to meet every single one of you and hope everyone has a fruitful debate! Do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions or concerns!
