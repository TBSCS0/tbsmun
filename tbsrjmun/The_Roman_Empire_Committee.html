    Santiago Iturri (TBSRJ)
    Head Chair

    Hi, my name is Santiago Iturri and I am a junior at EARJ. I couldn’t be more excited for this year's TBSRJMUN. I am honoured to be the Head Chair of the Roman Empire Committee and work alongside Lucas. I’ve spent the past few months dedicating myself to this committee and I’m certain it will be a terrific experience for any participants. It’s because of our joint efforts and hard work that I can promise that any delegate will enjoy their time debating Rome’s most prominent issues. MUN holds a meaningful place in my heart. It’s helped me grow in confidence, world knowledge, and connections. For me, it’s been an incredible catalyst for my development as the person I am today. Through every conference and committee, I try to share this experience and allow other delegates to feel the same way I do. If you have any questions or concerns, don’t hesitate to contact me. Let’s have a great conference together, I can’t wait to meet you all!
