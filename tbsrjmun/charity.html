Projeto Refúgio

All money collected through our donations, Chair Dares, and Love Mail will be donated to Projeto Refúgio! Learn more about their amazing work:

Projeto Refúgio is a charity dedicated to assisting Venezuelan refugee children who have arrived in Brazil. Their core objective is to empower these young refugees through education by equipping them with the language skills and cultural familiarity necessary for a successful integration into Brazilian society.

By offering Portuguese language lessons and insights into local customs, they aim to pave the way for a smoother adaptation and improved future employment opportunities for Venezuelan refugee children. Additionally, they extend a helping hand by providing essential items such as clothing, household necessities, and toys, aiming to alleviate some of the challenges they face.
