    Bruna Caillaux Di Napoli (TBSRJ)
    Head Chair

    Hello, delegates, my name is Bruna Caillaux Di Napoli and I am currently a Junior at TBSRJ Urca. I am honoured and delighted to be serving as Head Chair for the Historical Security Council. MUN has been an extremely important part of my life for over six years, and after so many conferences, I am still learning so much about the power of debate, the importance of listening, and the reach of human creativity. These skills will be especially important this year as we will be exploring very relevant and crucial moments of history, recontextualised through MUN. I hope to create an atmosphere of fluid debate, respect, and diplomacy. Yet still retaining the passion that turns the tide of argumentation. Part of my love for MUN is a love of language and so outside it. I can usually be found reading, writing, or watching a good movie. Part of the reason I adore the Historical Security Council is that I am a history buff and I truly believe that looking through our past is the only way to alter our future. I can’t wait to meet all of you and get the chance to get to know you better! If you have any questions at all, don’t hesitate to contact me.
