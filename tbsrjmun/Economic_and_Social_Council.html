    Lucia Melcon (St.Paul's School)
    Head Chair

    Hi, my name is Lucia Melcon and I am a Sophomore at St Paul’s School. I am extremely honoured to be head-charing this year’s Economic and Social Council at TBSRJMUN. Like many others, MUN has completely transformed my self-confidence and has helped me grow from a timid delegate into an enthusiastic and driven individual. As a chair, I will try to help those who are struggling with the inevitable initial doubt and nerves, as I have also been there. I hope my delegates feel as passionate about the committee’s topics as I do and that we have a fruitful debate. Aside from MUN, my other hobbies include cooking, running and watching Netflix. If you have any questions or doubts before the conference, please contact me!

    lucia.melconcarrasco@gmail.com

    Eduardo Chen (St. Nicholas School)
    Chair

    Hi, my name is Eduardo Chen, and I am a rising Senior at St. Nicholas School Pinheiros. I am beyond honoured to be chairing the Economic and Social Council Committee at TBSRJMUN XXI! When I first began doing MUN, I was an extremely quiet delegate that wasn’t sure if he would continue debating. However, the more conferences that I participated in, the stronger my passion for debate and love for MUN grew. While participating in conferences, I have met amazing people and forged incredible bonds with others. I hope that I can make this edition of TBSRJMUN unforgettable! If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out!
